What it does

Built for the way your phone actually gets messy

It catches files the moment they arrive

Take a screenshot or finish a download and FileFlow flags it, even when the app is closed. Star it to keep it, or let your retention rule quietly handle the rest.

Swipe to clean

Review files like a deck of cards. Right to keep, left to queue, then confirm the whole batch at once.

See before you delete

Every photo and video shows a real thumbnail, so you never lose the wrong file to a cryptic name.

Rules that fit your folders

Screenshots gone in a week. Documents kept for a month. Camera roll never touched. You set the limits.

Private by design

No server, no account, no uploads. Your files and your cleanup history never leave the phone.

A record of what went

The recycle log and analytics show exactly what was removed and how much space you got back.

Questions

Good to know

Why is it not on the Play Store yet?

It is in review. The beta gives you the complete app today while that approval finishes.

Is it safe to install?

Yes. The APK is signed and served directly from our GitHub releases. The first time, Android asks you to allow installs from your browser, which you can turn back off afterwards.

Does FileFlow upload my files?

No. There is no server and no account. Everything runs on your device, and your files and history stay there.

What permissions does it need?

All files access, so it can watch folders and remove the files you choose. Notifications are optional.

Will it delete something by accident?

Deletion is never silent. You swipe or confirm a batch, starred files are always protected, and you set how long each folder keeps files.
